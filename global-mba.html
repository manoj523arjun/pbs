Course Overview

In the midst of today's rapid-fire iteration, innovation and growth, entrepreneurs and business leaders in the 21st century need to do more than simply keep up. Global MBA gives high- achieving professionals the skills they need to lead organisations and manage a range of critical functions in a fast-paced, global business landscape.

Career Opportunities

Whether you are looking to accelerate your career, change direction or set up your own company, the Global MBA will give you the knowledge and transferable skills you need to succeed.

Our dedicated Postgraduate Careers Service knows that your objectives are ambitious and unique. Expect to boost your self-awareness and be challenged by experienced career advisors who will support you throughout your journey - from first steps to final offer and negotiation. You can focus on the career in front of you when you have all this behind you.

Entry requirements

  • Official transcripts from all degree-granting institutions attended
  • Academic transcripts and certificates of the 3 years of high school
  • Passport Copies
  • Curriculum vitae (CV)/Resume
  • 12 to 36 months of Work experience
  • SOP/Personal Statement
  • Two academic recommendation letters
  • In addition, for non-French speakers, an Official French Test Certificate equivalent to B2 level in French depends on the specialisation
  • In addition, for non-English speakers, an Official English Test Certificate equivalent to B2 depends on the specialisation
