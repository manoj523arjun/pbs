Course Overview

Learning French can improve your career development

You are a company which has international collaborators?

You are an expatriate worker in France, a foreign employee, a foreigner changing careers or seeking for a job?

Understanding and mastering the usage of the French language is a very important issue for your career path. It determines:

  • Your success
  • Your integration within your team or trading partners
  • Your independence
  • Your job security
  • Your promotion and career development

Learn business French with a French business language course today and boost your results in the business world. Learning business French can strengthen relationships with French speaking companies, boosting international trade and giving you a competitive edge.

Our French language training can be adjusted to any ability level, can be designed to target specific corporate skills, and can be set to a pace suitable for your team.

French Course Content

Your business French courses will be highly bespoke according to exactly what the learners need to learn and for what purpose. ESB will ensure that the correct areas are covered.

As a general guide, some popular areas and business skills to be covered include:

  • Job-specific vocabulary related to the sector of the business and role of the learner.
  • French language skills for presentations and speeches
  • French writing skills specifically for business writing
  • French oral skills for negotiations
  • French written and conversational skills for meetings
  • French pronunciation and accent reduction
