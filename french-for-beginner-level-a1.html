Course Overview

These lessons are designed to optimise oral expression and comprehension and to enable you to communicate with ease in various real-life situations.

They are aimed at all those wanting to make fast progress in French and listening and speaking are strongly encouraged (discussions about the news, using French newspapers, role plays etc.).

The objective of this course is to enable the student to participate in simple conversations, improve his or her communication strategies and understand audio documentation.

Academic Curriculum

Communication objectives

describe a timetable

talk about facts in the past

talk about one's professional experience

talk about the future

express obligation

express opinion

give advice

compare objects, compare people

request information

ask someone to do something

express cause and consequence

describe qualities

Grammatical objectives

irregular verbs in the present tense

futur proche (near future) and futur simple (simple future) tenses

passé composé (present perfect) tense with common verbs

pronominal verbs

modal verbs

polite requests in the conditional mood

comparison

partitive articles

Progression routes

  • Students will apply knowledge of linguistic elements of the French language accurately for specific communication needs
  • Students will apply language learning skills and strategies as a vehicle for personal, cognitive, and social development.
