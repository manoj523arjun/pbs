Course Overview

These lessons are designed to optimise oral expression and comprehension and to enable you to communicate with ease in various real-life situations.

They are aimed at all those wanting to make fast progress in French and listening and speaking are strongly encouraged (discussions about the news, using French newspapers, role plays etc.).

These lessons are for students who are beginners. The objective of these lessons is to build a foundation of communicative competence to help students make themselves understood and to understand very simple situations in daily life.

Academic Curriculum

Communication objectives

enter into contact with someone

describe a person

describe daily activities

express one's preferences

situate oneself in time

describe past actions in a simple way

make and confirm an appointment

describe plans

Grammatical objectives

regular and basic verbs in the present tense

a few verbs in the passé composé (present perfect)

negation

futur proche (near future)

why/because

prepositions

demonstrative adjectives

qualifying adjective

Progression routes

  • Students will apply knowledge of linguistic elements of the French language accurately for specific communication needs
  • Students will apply language learning skills and strategies as a vehicle for personal, cognitive, and social development.
