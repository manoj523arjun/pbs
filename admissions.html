Academic Requirements

Undergraduate Program

Students can join the Bachelor in Management in Year 1 (for a 3-year course) or in the final year top-up degree.

  • First-year entry requirement: GCSE and A-Levels
  • Third-year entry requirements: Applicants for Direct Entry to this top-up program will be expected to have 120 ECTS credits or equivalent gained on a business-related program such as Foundation Degree, HND, or Advanced Diploma acceptable to the University.

Postgraduate Program

International students can join our Master's programs with the following requirements:

  • A minimum of a second-class honour's degree, or an equivalent professional qualification. You must also have a minimum of three years of work experience. If you don't have formal qualifications but can demonstrate significant managerial experience - normally a minimum of five years - then we would encourage you to apply.

How to apply

1

Step One

  • Fill the application form.
  • Academic transcripts of the 3 years of high school
  • Official transcripts from all degree-granting institutions attended
  • Passport Copies
  • Curriculum vitae (CV)/Resumes
  • SOP/Personal Statement
  • Two Academic Recommendation Letters
  • In addition, for non-French speakers, an Official French Test Certificate equivalent to B1/B2 level in French depends on the specialization.
  • For non-English speakers, an Official English Test Certificate equivalent to B1/B2 depending on the specialization.
