Course Overview

Why Choose this Course?

Studying Hospitality & Tourism Management opens doors to the largest and fast-growing sector of the economy.

In response to employers, studying Hospitality & Tourism Management here at ESB gives you the chance to study this subject in the context of management, strategy, marketing, enterprise, economics, and ethics.

You'll have the skills to manage operations and business across a range of industries including hospitality, tourism, and events.

Study Abroad

An opportunity for an amazing experience, which will help make you stand out from the crowd. With more and more companies working internationally, experience of living in another country can make a great impression on future employers.

General Objectives of the Training

This course offers you the opportunity to enhance your study and CV with a sandwich year abroad. The ESB has partnerships with UK and Canada Universities. If you study abroad third year of study, we'll ask you to make your decision in your second year, so there is plenty of time to think about it.

Career Opportunities

The Professional Training placement is an integral part of our courses. Our prestigious partner organisations provide opportunities for you to gain valuable experience in a professional working environment, increasing your confidence, skills and employability.

You can choose to spend your placement in the France, Europe or even further afield.

Some examples of organisations that participate in this scheme include:

  • Langham Hotel
  • Dorchester Collection
  • Four Seasons Hotels
  • Hilton Worldwide
  • The Savoy
  • The Hospitality Company

Entry requirements

  • Academic transcripts of the last three years of high school
  • Passport Copies
  • Curriculum vitae (CV)/Resume
  • SOP/Personal Statement
  • Two academic recommendation letters
  • In addition, for non-French speakers, an Official French Test Certificate equivalent to B1 level in French depends on the specialisation.
  • In addition, for non-English speakers, an Official English Test Certificate equivalent to B1 depends on the specialisation.
